Scottish Myths & Legends

Fear Liath

Origin: Scottish Highlands

Behavior: A mysterious being that haunts climbers in the Cairngorm Mountains, instilling deep fear.

Cu Sith & Cat Sith

Origin: Scottish Folklore

Behavior: The Cu Sith is a green, wolf-like creature, and the Cat Sith is a spectral cat said to steal souls.

Wulver

Origin: Shetland Islands

Behavior: A benevolent wolf-like creature who guides lost travelers and leaves fish for poor families.

Fairy

Origin: Scottish Lore

Behavior: Known for their beauty and trickery, fairies interact with humans in both helpful and harmful ways.
